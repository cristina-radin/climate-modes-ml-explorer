Main view

Use the menu on the left to select ocean depth layers and explore global climate variability regions. You can see the spatial structure of each region, understand how different regions interact, and focus on areas of interest worldwide.



Interactive charts

Click on a region to inspect its properties.



Data download

All datasets used in the study are available for download via the “Download” button. This allows you to explore the data yourself, reproduce analyses, or use it in your own research.


Tips

  • Use the layer selector to compare different depths and understand vertical variability.
  • Zoom and pan on maps to focus on specific areas.
  • Explore multiple regions to see patterns and correlations between global climate modes.
